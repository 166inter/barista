Discover your inner barista

Learn the in's and out's of espresso making and milk pouring at home with your own machine or learn inside a professional cafe. Lesson plans are flexible and unique to your skill level. Ideal for the aspiring home barista or someone looking to work and make a better first impression at a specialty coffee place.

Life is too short to drink bad coffee. Third wave coffee is becoming increasingly popular as an industry and culture. Coffee is a rewarding craft to learn and a simple way to get in touch the finer things in life.

A great way to learn to make better coffee is by supervised practice. Anyone can learn to improve their skill and be more consistent by adjusting a variety of different variables.


Private and group lessons are available. A 10% discount is available for students. Email baristanic@gmail.com or message (438) 969-2905 for more information or to arrange for a lesson.

About Barista Nic

I am a student at the University of Windsor with a couple years' experience making specialty coffee. Having worked as a Barista in both Berlin and Montreal, I have explored the scene in order to improve my skill and appreciation for coffee. Now I am pleased to give you the tools and techniques for you to make great-tasting and great-looking coffee!
